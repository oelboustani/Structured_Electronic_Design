    Drive capabilities

    We choose the minimum length $L = 180 \text{ nm}$ and we iterate to find the width for which the ouput clips at approximately $350 \text{ mV}$ which is needed for the load. We take the width of the PMOS 4 times higher than the width of the NMOS because the PMOS has lower mobility. We arrived at $W_N = 180\;\mu\text{m}$ and $W_P = 720 \mu m$. From the transient simulation results, we can conclude that the stage satisfies the driving requirements. The output is plotted across the $50 \;\Omega$ load.
