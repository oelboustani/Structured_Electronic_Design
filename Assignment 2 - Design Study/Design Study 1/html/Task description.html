Task description

General Task

Perform a design study on different architectures for the implementation of the active antenna: $V_{DS}$, $I_{DS}$, $W$ and $L$.

1. Requirements

The antenna must drive a load of $50 $ with $1 mW$ while staying within the supply voltage of 1.8 V.

The noise floor must be below: $25*10^-25 V/(m^2*Hz)$

The 1/f-noise corner freqeuency must be below $100 kHz$.

The 1dB-compression point must be beyond $1 mW$.

The intermodeulation product within the signal band must be below $-50 dBm$.

2. The simple CS-stage -- Approach

Find whether drive capability and noise performance can be met with a single CS-stage.

Probe whether the distortion parameters are met with the design chosen
